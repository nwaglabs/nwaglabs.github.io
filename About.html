INTRO

I work with clients of all sizes to build brands that connect and websites that convert. My approach blends creativity with clarity, ensuring every project delivers meaningful, lasting impact. Whether launching a new product or refreshing an established presence, I bring sharp thinking, attention to detail, and a passion for design that moves people.

I'm Jamie, A digital designer based in Manchester

With over eight years of experience in UI and graphic design, I've had the privilege of collaborating with startups, agencies, and global brands across a range of industries. My background in visual storytelling allows me to craft thoughtful, intuitive experiences that not only look great but function beautifully. I believe good design isn’t just about aesthetics — it’s about solving real problems and elevating how people interact with a brand.

I’m constantly exploring new tools, trends, and technologies to stay ahead of the curve, blending tried-and-true principles with modern execution. Whether I’m sketching concepts, refining interfaces, or diving into development, I stay hands-on throughout the process. I’m passionate about working closely with clients and teams to turn ideas into bold, effective solutions — the kind that resonate with audiences and make an impact.
