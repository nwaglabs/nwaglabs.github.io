CLIENT

OVERVIEW

Hardscape Surfaces is a specialist UK provider of high-quality stonework solutions, delivering standout paving and hard landscaping projects for over two decades. From major stadiums to public parks, the company works with architects, contractors, and local authorities to transform spaces through expert craftsmanship, innovative design, and premium materials.

MY ROLES

  • Market Research
  • UI design
  • UX design

The Challenge

Hardscape Surfaces needed a website that reflected the strength, precision, and visual appeal of their stonework projects across the UK. From football stadiums to public parks, their work is both large-scale and highly detailed — but their previous site didn’t communicate that. They wanted a bold digital presence that not only showcased their extensive portfolio but also highlighted the people and expertise behind the craft.

The Solution

I designed a website that puts their work and team front and center. Using strong typographic choices, a dark but refined colour palette drawn from their brand, and full-width imagery and video, I created a visually immersive experience. The layout balances impact and clarity, making space for project visuals while also weaving in the human story behind the work. Subtle motion and thoughtful content hierarchy guide the user through the site, from striking visuals to in-depth case studies.

The Outcome

The finished site gives Hardscape Surfaces a bold and confident digital identity. It showcases the scale and quality of their work with impact, while offering a glimpse into the team and values behind the brand. The site has received positive feedback both internally and from clients, strengthening their credibility and aligning their online presence with the high standard of their physical work.
