CLIENT

Watercooling UK

OVERVIEW

WatercoolingUK is a specialist PC hardware retailer based in Manchester, UK, established in 2004. They are known for offering one of the largest selections of water-cooling components in the country, catering to gaming enthusiasts, system builders, and performance PC users. With a strong focus on quality, expert support, and fast delivery, WatercoolingUK has built a solid reputation in the custom PC community.

MY ROLES

  • Market Research
  • UI design
  • UX design

The Challenge

WatercoolingUK approached us with a clear objective: to redesign their user interface with a fresh, modern look that stood out in the competitive PC hardware space. Their existing platform had a strong user base and a vast product catalogue, but the visual design and overall UX had become outdated. Our challenge was to evolve the interface without compromising the familiarity long-time users relied on. The new design needed to feel both high-performance and approachable—reflecting the technical expertise of the brand while also appealing to newcomers to PC customisation.

In addition to updating the visual language, we were tasked with rethinking the structure and presentation of key features. This included streamlining the navigation for easier browsing, improving search and filter functionality, and enhancing the overall shopping experience. WatercoolingUK also wanted to introduce new capabilities—such as richer product comparison tools and a more integrated support experience—without overwhelming the user. Balancing innovation with usability became a central focus throughout the redesign process.

The Solution

To modernise the WatercoolingUK interface, we developed a fresh, clean design system that placed emphasis on usability, visual clarity, and brand personality. The new UI introduced a more intuitive layout, updated typography, and a refined colour palette that reflected the technical, performance-driven nature of the brand. We reorganised the navigation structure to make product discovery smoother and enhanced the filtering system to support faster, more precise browsing—especially important given the extensive product range. Key features such as product specs, reviews, and compatibility information were surfaced more intelligently to reduce friction throughout the shopping experience.

One of the standout additions was the development of a unique one-page checkout system. Designed to simplify the process without sacrificing detail, this streamlined flow allowed customers to review their order, apply discounts, select shipping options, and input payment—all in one responsive, easy-to-follow view. The layout was carefully crafted to maintain clarity across desktop and mobile, ensuring no usability was lost when transitioning between devices. The result was a more efficient, less cluttered checkout experience that still gave users full control and confidence when completing their purchase.

The Outcome

The final design for WatercoolingUK reflects a bold step forward for the brand—balancing a modern, performance-driven aesthetic with a clear and intuitive user experience. The refreshed UI brings greater consistency and clarity across the platform, with a restructured layout that simplifies browsing, product discovery, and access to technical information. Every design decision was made to support the needs of both seasoned PC builders and newcomers, without overwhelming users with complexity.

A key highlight of the redesign is the new one-page checkout system. This streamlined flow presents all essential steps—order review, discounts, shipping, and payment—in a single, responsive layout that transitions smoothly across devices. The result is a checkout experience that feels fast and straightforward while still providing users with all the details they need. With the groundwork now in place, the platform is ready to deliver an elevated e-commerce experience that aligns with WatercoolingUK’s reputation in the custom PC space.
