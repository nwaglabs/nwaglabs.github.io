Boat Charter

DATE

2024

CLIENT

Boat Charter Ibiza

OVERVIEW

Boat Charter Ibiza is a premium boat rental company offering a curated selection of luxury yachts, speedboats, and sailing vessels across the stunning coastline of Ibiza. Catering to both small private groups and larger parties, the company provides fully staffed charters for day trips, sunset cruises, and multi-day adventures. With a focus on comfort, style, and personalised service, Boat Charter Ibiza delivers unforgettable experiences on the water.

MY ROLES

  • Art Direction
  • Branding
  • UI design
  • UX design

The Challenge

Boat Charter Ibiza approached me to create a full brand identity and digital presence that would reflect the luxury and exclusivity of their offering. As a new business entering a competitive market, they needed a visual identity that felt high-end yet approachable, appealing to a broad audience of tourists and locals seeking premium experiences on the water.

In addition to the branding, the challenge was to design a website that not only captured the aspirational feel of chartering a private yacht in Ibiza, but also included a practical, user-friendly booking system. This system needed to handle detailed charter requests—factoring in boat size, number of guests, and onboard staff—while maintaining a smooth and elegant user experience.

The Solution

To establish a strong luxury presence, I designed a refined brand identity centred around a sleek, modern logo, an elegant color palette, and sophisticated typography that reflected the high-end nature of the service. This visual language was carried through to the website, creating a seamless and aspirational brand experience from first click to final booking.

The website design focused on clarity and ease of use, with a minimalist layout that showcased the fleet through large imagery and engaging content. I also designed a custom booking interface that allowed users to select their ideal vessel, specify guest numbers, and include optional crew or services. The result was a visually stunning and highly functional platform that balanced style with usability.

The Outcome

The final brand and website successfully positioned Boat Charter Ibiza as a premium, trustworthy provider in the luxury charter market. The clean, elegant design helped build instant credibility with users, while the intuitive booking system made it easy for potential customers to plan and personalise their experience.

The result is a cohesive and memorable brand presence that communicates quality and exclusivity, while supporting the company’s goals of attracting high-end clientele and streamlining the booking process. The project laid a strong foundation for growth, helping Boat Charter Ibiza make a confident entrance into a competitive market.
