CLIENT

Vista Engineering

OVERVIEW

Vista Engineering is a leading UK manufacturer of builders' metalwork, with over 50 years of experience in producing high-quality products for the construction industry. Specialising in wall ties, windposts, masonry support systems, angle beads, and joist hangers, Vista Engineering is committed to delivering innovation, quality, and reliability to its customers.

MY ROLES

  • Market Research
  • UI design
  • UX design

The Challenge

Vista Engineering approached our agency to redesign and modernise their online presence to better reflect their long-standing reputation in the industry. The website needed to showcase their extensive product range, streamline the customer experience, and improve functionality for both their B2B clients and internal teams.

Additionally, Vista Engineering required a more user-friendly interface and improved product categorisation to make it easier for customers to find the right products.

The Solution

We implemented an advanced product catalogue system, allowing Vista Engineering to showcase their comprehensive product range with detailed specifications and images. The system also includes filters and sorting options to enable visitors to find products based on their specific needs, such as size, material, and application type.

Focusing on an intuitive user journey, we redesigned the website’s structure to ensure easy navigation for both desktop and mobile users. Clear calls to action, an improved search functionality, and easy access to product information made the browsing experience more efficient and user-friendly.

The Outcome

The new Vista Engineering website successfully met the company's goals of showcasing their products, enhancing the user experience, and providing a more efficient platform for their customers. The custom WordPress solution provided Vista Engineering with the flexibility to manage content, update product listings, and track customer interactions seamlessly.

The result is a modern, responsive website that reinforces Vista Engineering’s position as an industry leader while also offering a more streamlined experience for their users.
